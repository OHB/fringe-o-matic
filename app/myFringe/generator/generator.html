Step 3: Generate a schedule

When you click Generate Schedule, this tool will generate a schedule for you while doing the following:

  1. trying to fit as many shows in as possible
  2. ensuring you have enough time to get to every show
  3. attempting to group shows to reduce travel between locations

We won't touch the already on your calendar, nor will we try to place 2 shows at the same time.

You'll have an opportunity to review the schedule before it is saved.
